My Journey With The FOC

  • May 2021: I joined the FOC as an intern working in the TPMO (Technology Project Management Office)
  • April 2022: I graduated from Oakland University and joined the FOC as a full-time TM working as a TechOps Project Coordinator in the TPMO

Why I love working at the FOC

From my first day as an intern working with the TPMO, I have truly felt welcomed and valued as a team member. I’ve had the opportunity to work on huge technology projects as an intern and project coordinator, such as Project Voyager (name change from QL > RM), the very first project I worked on at the FOC. The FOC has had my back as a student when I needed support and has continued to support me in my growth. I love the FOC!
